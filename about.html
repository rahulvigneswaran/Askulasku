The Inspiration

There's a joyous Tamil song called Asku Lasku (ஆஸ்கு லாஸ்கு). What makes it unforgettable is a single idea hiding inside its lyrics: the word love gets sung in language after language — Spanish, French, Hindi, Arabic, Japanese — the same feeling, dressed in every tongue spoken on Earth.

That moment — hearing one word bloom through dozens of scripts and sounds — is pure magic. It makes you feel how universal certain things really are. No matter which language you grew up speaking, love sounds like love.

This website is a small tribute to that idea: what if you could take any phrase at all and watch it travel the world in the same way?

What Asku Lasku Does

Type a word or phrase in English. The site translates it into 30+ major world languages in real time, then animates each translation across the screen — one language at a time, with a different motion for each.

Every transition is deliberate: a rise, a zoom, a blur-to-focus, a cascade from above. The aurora background shifts colour with each language, and tiny particles drift across the scene. The whole thing is designed to feel alive.

When you find a phrase that moves you, hit Export to download it as an animated GIF or WebM video and share it wherever you like.
